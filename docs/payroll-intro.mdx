---
title: "Gusto Embedded's Payroll APIs"
description: "APIs to configure and process accurate payroll at the right cadence"
---
Gusto Embedded's payroll APIs allow users to configure everything needed to process accurate payroll at the right cadence. Use the payroll APIs to complete regular payroll, configure 2-day & 4-day ACH, create company specific earnings, run off-cycle payroll, create historical payroll, and create pay schedules. To save on development time and accelerate time to market, you can also utilize our growing list of payroll Flows end-to-end or modularize the Flow by utilizing only the specific steps that you need.
