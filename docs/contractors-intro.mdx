---
title: "Gusto Contractor APIs"
description: "Onboard, manage, and pay 1099 contractors efficiently"
---
Based on the payroll patterns of Gusto's 200k+ customer base, 65% of businesses pay 1099 contractors.

Gusto Embedded's contractor APIs allow users to onboard, manage, and pay tax-exempt employees (1099 contractors per IRS regulations). Use the contractors APIs to allow users to onboard new contractors, file 1099 paperwork, and pay contractors alongside other employees. To save on development time and accelerate time to market, you can also utilize our prebuilt contractor onboarding Flow end-to-end or modularize the Flow by utilizing only the specific steps that you need.
